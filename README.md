# 🚀 Momentum AI

> Transform deadlines into momentum through AI-powered planning, risk prediction, adaptive scheduling, and productivity coaching.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

**The Last-Minute Life Saver**

Students, professionals, and freelancers often miss deadlines because traditional productivity tools rely on passive reminders that are easy to ignore.

Momentum AI proactively helps users prioritize, plan, recover, and complete important tasks before deadlines are missed.

---

## 🎯 Solution Overview

Momentum AI is an AI-powered productivity companion that:

- Prioritizes tasks intelligently
- Creates actionable execution plans
- Predicts deadline risks
- Adapts when users fall behind
- Provides emergency rescue strategies
- Delivers personalized productivity coaching
